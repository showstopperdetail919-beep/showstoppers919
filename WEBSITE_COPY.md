## 6. FAQ COPY

### Section Headline
**Questions? We've Got Answers.**

**Where do you service?**
We serve Durham, Raleigh, Cary, Chapel Hill, Morrisville, Hillsborough, and the surrounding Triangle area. We come to your home, office, or preferred location.

**Do I need to be home during the detail?**
Not necessarily, but we do need access to your vehicle and ideally a water source for wash services. Just let us know the details when you book.

**How long does each service take?**
A Basic Mobile Wash takes about 30–45 minutes. Gold Details run about 1–2 hours. Diamond Details typically take 3–4 hours depending on vehicle size and condition.

**What forms of payment do you accept?**
We accept cash, check, Zelle, Cash App, and other common payment methods. A deposit is collected when you book, and the remaining balance is due at the time of service.

**Where is window tint performed?**
Window tint is done at a specific location we provide after your booking is confirmed. It's not a mobile service — but we make the process easy.

**What if I need to cancel or reschedule?**
We understand things come up. Please give us at least 24 hours' notice to cancel or reschedule. Deposits are non-refundable for cancellations made with less than 24 hours' notice. See our full policy below.

**Do you detail RVs, boats, or fleet vehicles?**
Absolutely. These require a custom quote based on vehicle size and condition. Reach out through our booking form or give us a call and we'll get back to you within 24 hours.
